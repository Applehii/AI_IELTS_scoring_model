IELTS Writing Task 1 Overview:
- Task 1 requires summarizing and reporting visual information such as charts, tables, maps, and processes.
- The purpose is to describe key features, highlight main trends or stages, and make comparisons where relevant.
- Task 1 does NOT require giving opinions or personal interpretation.
- Writing must be factual, accurate, and focused on the information provided.
- A clear overview of the main features is essential for a good score.
