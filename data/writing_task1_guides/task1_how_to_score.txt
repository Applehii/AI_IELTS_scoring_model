How Task 1 is evaluated:
- TR (Task Achievement): Whether the candidate summarized key features, trends, changes, and comparisons.
- CC (Coherence & Cohesion): Whether ideas are logically structured, with proper paragraphing and linking.
- LR (Lexical Resource): Use of appropriate academic vocabulary, comparisons, quantifiers, and variation.
- GRA (Grammar Range & Accuracy): Accurate use of tenses, comparatives, complex sentences, and passive forms.

Common requirements:
- Identify the most significant trends or categories.
- Describe changes over time using appropriate tenses.
- Avoid unnecessary details unless they support key patterns.
