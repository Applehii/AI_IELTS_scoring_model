[QUESTION]
Watching TV has both advantages and disadvantages. Discuss both views.

[SUMMARY]
TV helps people relax and learn new information from news or documentaries. Children can watch educational shows.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 5
TV helps people relax and learn new information from news or documentaries. Children can watch educational shows. But many people spend too much time watching TV, and this affects their health. Kids may also watch violent programs. So TV has benefits but also dangers.

[OVERVIEW]
Information and entertainment but harmful if overused.
