[QUESTION]
Using public transport has advantages and disadvantages. Discuss both.

[SUMMARY]
Public transport is usually cheaper than driving a car. It reduces traffic jams because more people share one vehicle.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]

Public transport is usually cheaper than driving a car. It reduces traffic jams because more people share one vehicle. However, buses and trains are often crowded, especially during rush hours. They may also be late, which makes people annoyed. In conclusion, public transport helps save money but has some problems.

[OVERVIEW]
Cheap but crowded and unreliable.
