[QUESTION]
Some students prefer studying in groups. Discuss the advantages and disadvantages.

[SUMMARY]
Studying in groups allows students to share ideas and understand lessons better. They can also help each other with homework.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 5
Studying in groups allows students to share ideas and understand lessons better. They can also help each other with homework. However, some groups are not effective because students talk too much or get distracted. Too many opinions may also confuse them. Therefore, group study is helpful but not always successful.

[OVERVIEW]
Good for sharing ideas but may cause distraction.
