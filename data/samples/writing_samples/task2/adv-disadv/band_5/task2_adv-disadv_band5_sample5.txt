[QUESTION]
Many parents give their children smartphones. What are the advantages and disadvantages?

[SUMMARY]
Smartphones help parents keep in contact with their children easily. Kids can also use phones to study with apps.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 5
Smartphones help parents keep in contact with their children easily. Kids can also use phones to study with apps. But too much phone use can affect their eyes and reduce their time for homework. Children may also play games for many hours. So smartphones can be both useful and harmful.

[OVERVIEW]
Useful for communication but harmful for study habits.
