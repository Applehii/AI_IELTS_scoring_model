[QUESTION]
Many people eat fast food. What are the advantages and disadvantages?

[SUMMARY]
Fast food is quick and convenient for busy people. It is also cheap compared to restaurants.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 5
Fast food is quick and convenient for busy people. It is also cheap compared to restaurants. But fast food is unhealthy because it has a lot of fat and salt. Eating it often can cause health problems. So people should not depend on it too much.

[OVERVIEW]
Convenient but unhealthy.
