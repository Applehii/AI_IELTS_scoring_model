[QUESTION]
Many people work from home. What are the advantages and disadvantages?

[SUMMARY]
Working from home helps people save travel time and allows them to spend more time with family. It also reduces transport costs.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 5
Working from home helps people save travel time and allows them to spend more time with family. It also reduces transport costs. However, it can be difficult to concentrate at home because of noise or household tasks. Some workers also feel isolated without coworkers. So working from home is not perfect.

[OVERVIEW]
More time at home but less focus.
