[QUESTION]
Some parents let their children join many after-school activities. Discuss the advantages and disadvantages.

[SUMMARY]
After-school activities help children develop skills such as teamwork, creativity, and discipline. These activities also keep them active and prevent them from spending too much time on screens.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 6
After-school activities help children develop skills such as teamwork, creativity, and discipline. These activities also keep them active and prevent them from spending too much time on screens. On the other hand, having too many activities can make children stressed and tired. They may not have enough time to rest or complete homework. Therefore, after-school programs should be balanced.

[OVERVIEW]
Skill development vs. stress and lack of rest.
