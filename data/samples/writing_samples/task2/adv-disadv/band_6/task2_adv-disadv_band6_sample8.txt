[QUESTION]
Using bicycles has advantages and disadvantages. Discuss both.

[SUMMARY]
Cycling is good for health because it helps people exercise regularly. It is also environmentally friendly and reduces traffic jams.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 6
Cycling is good for health because it helps people exercise regularly. It is also environmentally friendly and reduces traffic jams. However, riding a bicycle can be dangerous in busy cities due to the lack of bike lanes. It is also not suitable for long distances or bad weather. Overall, bicycles are useful but not ideal in every situation.

[OVERVIEW]
Healthy and eco-friendly but unsafe and limited.
