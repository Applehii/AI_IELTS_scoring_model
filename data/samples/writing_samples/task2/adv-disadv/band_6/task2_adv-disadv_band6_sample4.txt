[QUESTION]
Some students take a gap year before university. Discuss its advantages and disadvantages.

[SUMMARY]
A gap year gives students time to relax after stressful school years. They can travel, work, or join volunteer programs, which helps them gain real-life experience.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 6
A gap year gives students time to relax after stressful school years. They can travel, work, or join volunteer programs, which helps them gain real-life experience. However, some students may become lazy and lose motivation to study again. Moreover, a long break can make it harder to return to academic routines. Therefore, a gap year has both benefits and risks.

[OVERVIEW]
More experience but risk of losing study habits.
