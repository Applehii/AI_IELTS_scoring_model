[QUESTION]
Many people choose to travel abroad for holidays. What are the advantages and disadvantages?

[SUMMARY]
Travelling abroad allows people to experience new cultures and enjoy unique destinations that they cannot find in their home country.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 6
Travelling abroad allows people to experience new cultures and enjoy unique destinations that they cannot find in their home country. It can also help them become more open-minded. However, international trips are usually expensive, so some families cannot afford them. In addition, travellers may face language barriers or cultural misunderstandings. Overall, travelling overseas is exciting but also challenging.

[OVERVIEW]
Cultural exposure vs. high cost and communication issues.
