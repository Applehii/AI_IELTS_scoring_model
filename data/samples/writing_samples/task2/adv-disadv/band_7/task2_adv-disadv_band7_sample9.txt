[QUESTION]
Using smartphones has advantages and disadvantages. Discuss both.

[SUMMARY]
Smartphones allow people to communicate instantly and access information anywhere. They also provide useful tools such as navigation and online banking.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 7
Smartphones allow people to communicate instantly and access information anywhere. They also provide useful tools such as navigation and online banking. However, smartphone addiction is becoming a serious issue, especially among teenagers. Excessive use can harm concentration and sleep quality. In conclusion, smartphones are essential in modern life but require responsible use.

[OVERVIEW]
Convenient and multifunctional but may lead to addiction.
