[QUESTION]
Some companies allow employees to work from home. Discuss the advantages and disadvantages.

[SUMMARY]
Working from home reduces commuting time and helps employees maintain a better work–life balance. Many people feel more comfortable and productive in their own space.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 7
Working from home reduces commuting time and helps employees maintain a better work–life balance. Many people feel more comfortable and productive in their own space. However, remote work can create communication problems, especially in jobs that require teamwork. Employees may also struggle with distractions at home. Therefore, while working from home is beneficial for many, it may not suit every profession.

[OVERVIEW]
Better balance and comfort, but possible communication and focus issues.
