[QUESTION]
Many people watch foreign movies and TV shows. What are the advantages and disadvantages?

[SUMMARY]
Foreign media helps people understand different cultures and improve their language skills. It also provides more entertainment choices than local productions.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 7
Foreign media helps people understand different cultures and improve their language skills. It also provides more entertainment choices than local productions. However, watching too much foreign content may reduce interest in domestic culture, which can affect the local entertainment industry. Some people may also develop unrealistic expectations from foreign lifestyles. In short, foreign media is enriching but may weaken cultural identity.

[OVERVIEW]
Cultural learning vs. reduced interest in local culture.
