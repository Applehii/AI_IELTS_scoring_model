[QUESTION]
Many people choose package holidays. What are the advantages and disadvantages?

[SUMMARY]
Package holidays are convenient because travel, accommodation, and activities are arranged in advance. This helps travellers save time and avoid stress.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 7
Package holidays are convenient because travel, accommodation, and activities are arranged in advance. This helps travellers save time and avoid stress. They are often cheaper than booking everything separately. However, package tours may limit personal freedom, as tourists must follow fixed schedules. Some packages may also include tourist traps that are overcrowded and expensive. Overall, package holidays are practical but may not suit independent travellers.

[OVERVIEW]
Convenient and cost-effective but restrict freedom.
