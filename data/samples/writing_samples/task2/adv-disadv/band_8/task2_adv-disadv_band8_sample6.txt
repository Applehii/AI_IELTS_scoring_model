[QUESTION]
Some students choose to study abroad. Discuss the advantages and disadvantages.

[SUMMARY]
Studying abroad provides access to high-quality education and allows students to become more independent. Exposure to different cultures helps them develop global awareness.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 8
Studying abroad provides access to high-quality education and allows students to become more independent. Exposure to different cultures helps them develop global awareness. Nevertheless, overseas education can be extremely expensive, creating long-term financial burdens. Cultural shock and homesickness may also hinder academic performance. Therefore, studying abroad offers transformative benefits but comes with significant emotional and financial challenges.

[OVERVIEW]
High-quality education vs. cultural shock and high cost.
