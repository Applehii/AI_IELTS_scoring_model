[QUESTION]
Many people enjoy watching foreign movies and TV programmes. What are the advantages and disadvantages?

[SUMMARY]
Foreign media exposes viewers to diverse cultures and global perspectives, which can broaden their understanding of the world. It also motivates language learning.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 8
Foreign media exposes viewers to diverse cultures and global perspectives, which can broaden their understanding of the world. It also motivates language learning. However, excessive consumption of foreign content may undermine local film industries and diminish cultural identity. People may begin to value foreign lifestyles more than their own, leading to cultural erosion. Thus, foreign media enriches cultural awareness but may weaken local traditions.

[OVERVIEW]
Encourages cultural learning but threatens local identity.
