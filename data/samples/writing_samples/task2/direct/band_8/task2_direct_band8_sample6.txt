[QUESTION]
Why do people volunteer in their communities? What benefits does volunteering provide?

[SUMMARY]
People volunteer for various reasons, including a desire to contribute to society and support those in need. Volunteering also provides a sense of fulfilment and purpose, as individuals feel they are making a positive impact.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 8
People volunteer for various reasons, including a desire to contribute to society and support those in need. Volunteering also provides a sense of fulfilment and purpose, as individuals feel they are making a positive impact. Some people volunteer to gain experience and develop new skills, especially young adults.
Volunteering benefits both communities and individuals. Communities receive valuable support in areas such as education, healthcare, and public services. Volunteers themselves develop empathy, leadership skills, and stronger social connections. These benefits make volunteering a meaningful activity for many people.
