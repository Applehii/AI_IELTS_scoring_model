[QUESTION]
Why do people enjoy reading books? How can reading improve people’s lives?

[SUMMARY]

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 8
Reading books provides entertainment and intellectual stimulation. Many people enjoy immersing themselves in stories or learning about new topics. Unlike watching videos, reading allows individuals to imagine scenes and characters, which can be deeply satisfying.
Reading also improves people’s lives in many ways. It strengthens language skills, enhances concentration, and promotes empathy by exposing readers to different perspectives. In the long term, regular reading contributes to better mental health and personal growth.
