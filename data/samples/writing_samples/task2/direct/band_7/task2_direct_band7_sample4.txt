Why do many people learn new skills online? What problems can occur?

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]

Many people choose online learning because it is convenient and affordable. Platforms like Coursera and Udemy allow learners to access a wide range of courses anytime. This flexibility is especially helpful for working adults who cannot attend traditional classes.
However, learning online can lead to a lack of motivation, as students often study alone without direct support from teachers. Technical issues, such as unstable internet connections, can also interrupt learning. Despite these problems, online education remains a valuable resource.
