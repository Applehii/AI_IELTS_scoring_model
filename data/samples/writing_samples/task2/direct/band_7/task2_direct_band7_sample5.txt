[QUESTION]
Why do people choose to keep pets? What responsibilities do pet owners have?

[SUMMARY]
People keep pets for emotional support and companionship. Pets help reduce stress and make people feel less lonely, especially for those living alone.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 7
People keep pets for emotional support and companionship. Pets help reduce stress and make people feel less lonely, especially for those living alone. Additionally, many families enjoy having pets to teach children responsibility and empathy.
Pet owners have several responsibilities. They must provide proper food, healthcare, and a safe living environment. Owners should also train their pets and ensure they do not disturb neighbours. Responsible pet ownership benefits both the animals and the community.
