[QUESTION]
Why are people becoming more interested in space exploration? What benefits does it bring?

[SUMMARY]

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]

People are more interested in space exploration because it represents human curiosity and technological progress. With new discoveries and private companies joining the space industry, space travel seems more achievable than ever.
The benefits of space exploration are significant. It helps scientists understand climate change by studying other planets, and new technologies developed for space missions often improve daily life, such as advanced satellites and communication tools. Although expensive, space exploration inspires innovation and global cooperation.
