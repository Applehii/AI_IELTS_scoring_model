[QUESTION]
Why do people enjoy doing sports? What benefits does this bring to society?

[SUMMARY]

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]

People enjoy sports because they help improve physical health and reduce stress. Team sports also allow people to socialise and build teamwork skills. For many individuals, sports are a way to disconnect from daily problems.
Sports benefit society in several ways. They encourage healthy lifestyles, which reduces the burden on healthcare systems. Major sports events also attract tourists and boost the economy. Overall, sports play an important role in community development.
