[QUESTION]
Why do many people feel stressed in modern society? What practical steps can individuals take to reduce stress?

[SUMMARY]
Stress has become pervasive in modern society due to increased workload, rising living costs, and the expectation of constant availability.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 9
Stress has become pervasive in modern society due to increased workload, rising living costs, and the expectation of constant availability. Social media also contributes by fostering unrealistic comparisons and amplifying public scrutiny. Collectively, these pressures create an environment where mental strain is almost inevitable.
To reduce stress, individuals can adopt evidence-based practices such as mindfulness meditation, regular exercise, and structured time management. Limiting exposure to digital distractions, prioritising restorative sleep, and nurturing supportive relationships are also effective. These practical steps, when combined, can significantly improve emotional resilience.
