[QUESTION]
Why is lifelong learning becoming increasingly important? How can people stay motivated to learn throughout life?

[SUMMARY]
Lifelong learning has gained importance because rapid technological advancement continually reshapes the job market. Skills that were valuable a decade ago may now be obsolete, requiring workers to adapt to new tools and knowledge.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 9
Lifelong learning has gained importance because rapid technological advancement continually reshapes the job market. Skills that were valuable a decade ago may now be obsolete, requiring workers to adapt to new tools and knowledge. Moreover, lifelong learning enhances cognitive health and helps people stay engaged in an ever-changing world.
To maintain motivation, individuals can set meaningful learning goals, engage in communities of practice, and track their progress through measurable milestones. Choosing subjects that spark genuine curiosity also sustains long-term engagement. By viewing learning as an evolving journey rather than a fixed destination, people remain motivated throughout their lives.
