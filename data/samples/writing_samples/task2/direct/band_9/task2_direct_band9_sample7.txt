[QUESTION]
Why are technological innovations becoming essential in education? What future developments can be expected?

[SUMMARY]

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 9
Technological innovations are increasingly vital in education because they personalise learning and expand access to high-quality resources. Interactive platforms allow students to learn at their own pace, while digital tools enable teachers to track progress more effectively. The shift toward blended learning has also demonstrated that technology can enhance rather than replace traditional teaching.
In the future, developments such as AI-powered tutoring, virtual reality classrooms, and adaptive learning systems are likely to become mainstream. These innovations will create more immersive and inclusive educational experiences. Although challenges remain, technology will undoubtedly shape the next generation of learning.
