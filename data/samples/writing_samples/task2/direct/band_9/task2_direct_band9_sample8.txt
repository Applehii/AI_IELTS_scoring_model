[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 9
Friendships are highly valued because they provide emotional support, companionship, and a sense of belonging. In times of difficulty, friends offer empathy and perspective that help individuals cope with challenges. Friendships also enrich life by creating shared experiences and fostering mutual growth.
Emotionally, friendships contribute to well-being by reducing stress and loneliness. Psychological research consistently shows that people with strong social connections are happier and more resilient. Moreover, friendships encourage healthier habits, such as regular exercise and open communication, which further improve mental health.
