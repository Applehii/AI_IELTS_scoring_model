[QUESTION]
Why do people value having free time? How does free time affect people’s lives?

[SUMMARY]

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]

People value free time because modern life is stressful. After long working hours, they want time to relax, meet friends, or enjoy hobbies. Free time helps people reduce stress and stay mentally healthy.
Free time also affects people’s lives in positive ways. It improves relationships when families spend time together. On the other hand, too much free time may make some people lazy or unproductive.
