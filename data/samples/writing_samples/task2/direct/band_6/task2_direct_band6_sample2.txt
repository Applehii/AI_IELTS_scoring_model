[QUESTION]
Why do people enjoy watching documentaries? What benefits do documentaries bring?

[SUMMARY]
People enjoy documentaries because they provide real information about the world. Many people like learning about history, animals, or science, and documentaries make these topics easy to understand.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]

People enjoy documentaries because they provide real information about the world. Many people like learning about history, animals, or science, and documentaries make these topics easy to understand. They are also more interesting than reading books for some viewers.
The benefits are significant. Documentaries can raise awareness about important issues, such as climate change or social problems. They also help people develop critical thinking because they present different viewpoints.
