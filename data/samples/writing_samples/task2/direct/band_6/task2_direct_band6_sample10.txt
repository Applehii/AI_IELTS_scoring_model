People choose online shopping because it is convenient and offers more choices than local stores. They can compare prices easily and save time.
However, this trend can cause several problems. Some products do not look the same as the pictures, and returning items can be difficult. Online shopping also reduces sales in traditional shops, which may lead to job losses.
