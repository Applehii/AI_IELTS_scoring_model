[QUESTION]
Why do many people prefer shopping online? What are the disadvantages of this trend?

[SUMMARY]
Many people shop online because it is convenient. They can buy things without leaving their house.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 5
Many people shop online because it is convenient. They can buy things without leaving their house. Online shops also have more choices and sometimes cheaper prices.
However, online shopping has disadvantages. Customers cannot check the product quality, and sometimes they receive the wrong item. Another problem is that online shopping may reduce sales in local stores.
