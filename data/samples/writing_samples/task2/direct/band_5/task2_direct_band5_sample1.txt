[QUESTION]
Why do many people eat unhealthy food? How can this habit be changed?

[SUMMARY]
Many people eat unhealthy food because it is cheap and easy to find. Fast food restaurants are everywhere, so people choose them when they are busy.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 5
Many people eat unhealthy food because it is cheap and easy to find. Fast food restaurants are everywhere, so people choose them when they are busy. Also, some people do not know how to cook healthy meals.
To change this habit, the government can educate people about healthy food. Schools should teach children to eat better. Restaurants can also offer more healthy choices. If these things happen, people may slowly improve their eating habits.
