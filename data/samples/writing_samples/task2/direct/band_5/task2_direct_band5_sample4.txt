[QUESTION]
Why do some people prefer online learning? What problems can it cause?

[SUMMARY]
Some people prefer online learning because it is flexible. They can study at home and save time on travelling.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]

Some people prefer online learning because it is flexible. They can study at home and save time on travelling. It is also cheaper than traditional classes for many students.
However, online learning can cause problems. Students may lose focus because there are many distractions at home. They also do not have direct communication with teachers, so they may not understand lessons well.
