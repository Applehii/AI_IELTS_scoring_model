[QUESTION]
Social media makes people less social. Do you agree or disagree?

[SUMMARY]
Although social media facilitates global communication, I believe it has reduced the quality of real-life interactions. Many users prioritise online engagement over face-to-face conversations, which weakens emotional bonds.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 8
Although social media facilitates global communication, I believe it has reduced the quality of real-life interactions. Many users prioritise online engagement over face-to-face conversations, which weakens emotional bonds. Nevertheless, social media can be beneficial for maintaining long-distance relationships. Ultimately, its impact depends on self-discipline, but current trends suggest a decline in meaningful social contact.
