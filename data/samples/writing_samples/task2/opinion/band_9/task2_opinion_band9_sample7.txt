[QUESTION]
Owning a car is essential. Do you agree or disagree?

[SUMMARY]

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 9
I disagree that car ownership is essential for modern life. In well-developed cities with efficient public transport systems, relying on a car is often impractical due to congestion and environmental restrictions. Conversely, in rural regions with limited infrastructure, owning a vehicle may be necessary. Therefore, the necessity of a car is highly context-dependent, and the claim that it is universally essential oversimplifies the diverse living environments people inhabit.
