[QUESTION]
School exams are the best way to judge students. Do you agree or disagree?

[SUMMARY]
Exams are an effective tool for measuring academic knowledge, but they should not be the only method. Exams assess memory and problem-solving skills, yet they fail to evaluate creativity, communication, and teamwork.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 7
Exams are an effective tool for measuring academic knowledge, but they should not be the only method. Exams assess memory and problem-solving skills, yet they fail to evaluate creativity, communication, and teamwork. Schools should combine exams with project-based assessments to gain a more accurate understanding of students’ strengths and weaknesses.

[OVERVIEW]
Reasonable and balanced.
