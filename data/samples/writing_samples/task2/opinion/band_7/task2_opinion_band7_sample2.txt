[QUESTION]
Some people believe that watching TV is harmful to children. Do you agree or disagree?

[SUMMARY]
I partly agree with this idea. Excessive TV watching can negatively affect children’s eyesight and limit their opportunities for physical development.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 7
I partly agree with this idea. Excessive TV watching can negatively affect children’s eyesight and limit their opportunities for physical development. However, educational programs can be highly beneficial, helping them learn language and general knowledge. Therefore, TV is not harmful by itself; the key is for parents to monitor content and limit screen time to a reasonable level.
