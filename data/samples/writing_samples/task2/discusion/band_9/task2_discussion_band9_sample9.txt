[QUESTION]
Some people think that older employees bring more value to a company, while others believe younger workers are more important. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

[SUMMARY]
Older employees are often valued for their experience, reliability, and leadership skills. They possess deep industry knowledge that helps companies avoid costly mistakes.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 9
Older employees are often valued for their experience, reliability, and leadership skills. They possess deep industry knowledge that helps companies avoid costly mistakes. On the other hand, younger workers bring fresh perspectives, technological expertise, and greater adaptability to change. They often drive innovation in fast-moving industries. In my opinion, companies benefit most from a balanced workforce where both age groups complement each other, contributing stability and creativity.
