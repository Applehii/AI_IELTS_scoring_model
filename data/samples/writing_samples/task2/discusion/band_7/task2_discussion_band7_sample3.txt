[QUESTION]
Some people say advertising encourages people to buy unnecessary products, while others argue it provides useful information. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

[SUMMARY]
Some argue that advertising makes people purchase items they do not really need by using persuasive visuals and emotional messages.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 7
Some argue that advertising makes people purchase items they do not really need by using persuasive visuals and emotional messages. This often leads to over-consumption. However, others believe advertisements offer helpful information about new products, allowing customers to make informed choices. In my opinion, advertising is beneficial when it is honest, but regulations should prevent misleading promotions to protect consumers.

[OVERVIEW]
Explains both sides clearly with a balanced opinion.
