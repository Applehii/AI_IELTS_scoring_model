[QUESTION]
Some people think that parents should control their children’s screen time, while others believe children should manage it themselves. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

[SUMMARY]
Many people argue that parents must regulate screen time because excessive use can affect children’s health and academic performance. They believe young people lack the discipline to limit themselves.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 7
Many people argue that parents must regulate screen time because excessive use can affect children’s health and academic performance. They believe young people lack the discipline to limit themselves. However, others think allowing children to control their own usage helps them develop independence and responsibility. In my view, parents should guide younger children, but teenagers can gradually be given more freedom with clear boundaries.
