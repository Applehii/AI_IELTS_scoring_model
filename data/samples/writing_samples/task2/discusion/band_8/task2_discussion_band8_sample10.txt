[QUESTION]
Some people believe that working from home is more effective, while others argue that office work is better. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

[SUMMARY]
Some people believe remote work increases productivity because employees can create comfortable environments and avoid commuting stress. Additionally, flexible schedules may improve work-life balance.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 8
Some people believe remote work increases productivity because employees can create comfortable environments and avoid commuting stress. Additionally, flexible schedules may improve work-life balance. On the other hand, others argue that office settings promote teamwork and clearer communication, which are difficult to maintain online. In my view, both systems have advantages, but a hybrid model combining flexibility with in-person collaboration is the most effective approach.
