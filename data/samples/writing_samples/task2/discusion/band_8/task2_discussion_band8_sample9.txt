[QUESTION]
Some people think competition is necessary for children, while others believe cooperation is more important. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

[SUMMARY]
Supporters of competition claim it motivates children to work harder and prepares them for real-world challenges. They argue competition builds resilience and ambition.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 8
Supporters of competition claim it motivates children to work harder and prepares them for real-world challenges. They argue competition builds resilience and ambition. However, others think cooperation teaches communication and teamwork, which are essential in modern workplaces. In my opinion, children need exposure to both approaches, but schools should prioritise cooperation to promote healthier social development and reduce unnecessary pressure.
