[QUESTION]
Some people believe that online learning is as effective as traditional classroom learning, while others disagree. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

[SUMMARY]
Supporters of online learning argue that it offers flexibility and accessibility, enabling students to learn at their own pace. Digital platforms also provide interactive materials that can enhance engagement.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 8
Supporters of online learning argue that it offers flexibility and accessibility, enabling students to learn at their own pace. Digital platforms also provide interactive materials that can enhance engagement. Conversely, critics claim that classroom learning is superior because it encourages direct communication, collaboration, and instant feedback from teachers. In my view, online education is highly effective for motivated learners, but traditional classrooms remain essential for younger students who need structure and social interaction.

Clear evaluation of strengths and limitations of both modes.
