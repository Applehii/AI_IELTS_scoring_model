[QUESTION]
More people are choosing eco-friendly lifestyles. Why is this happening? Is this a positive or negative development?

[SUMMARY]
The shift toward eco-friendly living is driven by increased environmental awareness and government campaigns promoting sustainability. People now recognise the consequences of climate change, such as extreme weather and pollution.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 8
The shift toward eco-friendly living is driven by increased environmental awareness and government campaigns promoting sustainability. People now recognise the consequences of climate change, such as extreme weather and pollution.
This change is highly positive because it reduces waste and promotes long-term environmental protection. However, eco-friendly products are still expensive, which may exclude lower-income groups. Despite this challenge, the overall trend is beneficial for society.
