[QUESTION]
Nowadays children spend much of their free time using electronic devices. Why is this? Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?

[SUMMARY]
Children increasingly use electronic devices because digital entertainment is highly engaging and widely accessible. Parents also rely on screens to keep children occupied due to busy work schedules.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 8
Children increasingly use electronic devices because digital entertainment is highly engaging and widely accessible. Parents also rely on screens to keep children occupied due to busy work schedules.
While technology can improve creativity and digital literacy, excessive screen time contributes to reduced physical activity and shorter attention spans. Overall, the disadvantages are more significant because long-term health and cognitive impacts outweigh short-term convenience.
