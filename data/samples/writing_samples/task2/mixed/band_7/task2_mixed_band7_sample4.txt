Some people frequently change their careers, while others stay in the same field for many years. Why do people change careers? Do you think this is a good idea?

[SUMMARY]

Career switching has become common because modern industries evolve quickly. Workers must update their skills and sometimes change paths to remain competitive. Others simply seek more meaningful work or better pay.
Changing careers can be rewarding because it allows people to discover their strengths. However, it may also result in instability and require significant retraining. I believe it is beneficial when people make informed decisions, not impulsive ones.
