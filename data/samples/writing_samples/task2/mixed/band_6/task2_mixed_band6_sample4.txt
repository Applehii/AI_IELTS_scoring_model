People change careers for several reasons. Some want higher salaries or better working conditions, while others search for jobs that match their interests. Modern workplaces also change quickly, so people need new skills. Career changes can be positive because they allow individuals to explore their strengths. However, switching too often may make it difficult to build expertise. I think career changes are beneficial when they are planned carefully.
