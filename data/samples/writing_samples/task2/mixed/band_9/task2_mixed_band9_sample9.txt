[QUESTION]
People increasingly rely on online news instead of printed newspapers. Why? Is this a positive or negative trend?

[SUMMARY]
Online news is preferred because it is immediate, interactive, and often free. Technological improvements allow users to receive updates tailored to their interests.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 9
Online news is preferred because it is immediate, interactive, and often free. Technological improvements allow users to receive updates tailored to their interests.
This shift is largely positive. It reduces paper waste, democratizes information access, and encourages media competition. The main drawback is misinformation, but with robust digital literacy programmes, this issue can be controlled.
