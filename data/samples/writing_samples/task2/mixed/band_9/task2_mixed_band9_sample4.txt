[QUESTION]
Many people are adopting eco-friendly lifestyles. Why? Is this a positive or negative development?

[SUMMARY]
Environmental concerns such as global warming and resource depletion have made eco-friendly habits more appealing. Governments and media campaigns increasingly highlight the urgency of sustainable living, influencing public behaviour.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]

Environmental concerns such as global warming and resource depletion have made eco-friendly habits more appealing. Governments and media campaigns increasingly highlight the urgency of sustainable living, influencing public behaviour.
This development is unquestionably positive. By reducing waste and carbon emissions, eco-conscious choices contribute directly to environmental preservation. While some sustainable products remain expensive, the long-term social and ecological gains far outweigh this limitation.
