[QUESTION]
Many companies target advertisements at children. Why? Is this a positive or negative trend?

[SUMMARY]
Children represent a powerful consumer group because they influence family purchasing decisions and form long-term brand loyalty. Companies exploit this by using vibrant imagery and persuasive characters to capture children’s attention.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]

Children represent a powerful consumer group because they influence family purchasing decisions and form long-term brand loyalty. Companies exploit this by using vibrant imagery and persuasive characters to capture children’s attention.
This trend is largely negative. Advertising can manipulate children’s emotions and promote unhealthy products such as sugary snacks or fast food. While educational campaigns may have some value, stricter regulations are required to prevent commercial exploitation.
