[QUESTION]
Air pollution is becoming worse in many cities. What problems and solutions?

[SUMMARY]
Air pollution leads to serious health problems. People may suffer from asthma or lung diseases because of dirty air.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 6
Air pollution leads to serious health problems. People may suffer from asthma or lung diseases because of dirty air. It also affects the environment, causing smog and damaging plants. To solve this, governments should limit car use and invest in green energy. Public transport can also be improved so people do not rely on private vehicles. By doing this, air quality will improve.

[OVERVIEW]
Air pollution harms health; reduce car use and use green energy.
