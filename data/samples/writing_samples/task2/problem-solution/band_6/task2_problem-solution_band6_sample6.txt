[QUESTION]
Many people suffer from poor work–life balance. What are the problems and solutions?

[SUMMARY]
Poor work–life balance can affect both health and relationships. People who work long hours often feel stressed and lack time to rest.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 6
Poor work–life balance can affect both health and relationships. People who work long hours often feel stressed and lack time to rest. They may also have little time for their families, which can weaken family bonds. One solution is for companies to offer flexible working hours or allow employees to work from home. Individuals should also learn to manage their time better and avoid unnecessary overtime. These actions may help improve work–life balance.

[OVERVIEW]
Stress and family issues; flexible work and time management help.
