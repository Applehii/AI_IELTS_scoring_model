[QUESTION]
Some people are addicted to social media. What problems and solutions?

[SUMMARY]
Social media addiction affects both mental health and productivity. People spend too much time online and become isolated from real-life relationships.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 6
Social media addiction affects both mental health and productivity. People spend too much time online and become isolated from real-life relationships. Students also lose focus on their studies. One solution is to limit screen time by using apps that control usage. Parents and schools can also educate young people about healthy online habits. These actions can reduce addiction.

[OVERVIEW]
Addiction harms mental health; screen limits help.
