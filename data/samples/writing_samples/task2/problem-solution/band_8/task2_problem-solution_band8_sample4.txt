[QUESTION]
Many adults suffer from sleep deprivation. What problems result from this, and what solutions can be offered?

[SUMMARY]
Sleep deprivation leads to a range of physical and cognitive issues. People who sleep too little are more prone to illnesses such as hypertension, and they often perform poorly at work due to reduced concentration.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 8
Sleep deprivation leads to a range of physical and cognitive issues. People who sleep too little are more prone to illnesses such as hypertension, and they often perform poorly at work due to reduced concentration. Chronic sleep loss can also affect emotional stability, leading to irritability and difficulty maintaining relationships.
A helpful solution is for companies to reduce excessive overtime and promote work-life balance through flexible scheduling. Individuals should also practise better sleep hygiene by limiting caffeine and avoiding screens before bedtime. These combined efforts can significantly improve sleep quality.

[OVERVIEW]
Lack of sleep harms health, productivity, and relationships; workplace policies and lifestyle changes help.
