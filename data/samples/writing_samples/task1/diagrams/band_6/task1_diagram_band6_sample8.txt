The paper-making process starts with logs being cut from trees. These logs are crushed into wood chips and mixed with water to form pulp. The pulp is pressed and then dried to create large sheets. The sheets are rolled and later cut into smaller pieces before packaging.

[OVERVIEW]
Wood is turned into paper by crushing, pulping and drying.
