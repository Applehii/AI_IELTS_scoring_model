Cocoa pods are collected from cacao trees and the beans are removed. These beans are fermented in baskets for several days and then dried under the sun. After drying, they are roasted in large ovens and crushed to separate the outer shells. The remaining cocoa mass is pressed and mixed with other ingredients to make chocolate.

[OVERVIEW]
Chocolate is made through harvesting, fermenting, drying, roasting and mixing.
