[QUESTION]
The diagram shows how rainwater is collected for drinking in an Australian town.

[SUMMARY]
Rainwater from rooftops is directed into collection gutters. It flows through pipes into a storage tank.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 6
Rainwater from rooftops is directed into collection gutters. It flows through pipes into a storage tank. The water is then transferred to a treatment facility where chemicals and filters are used to clean it. After treatment, the water is pumped into a supply system and delivered to houses for drinking.

[OVERVIEW]
Rainwater is collected, stored, cleaned and distributed.
