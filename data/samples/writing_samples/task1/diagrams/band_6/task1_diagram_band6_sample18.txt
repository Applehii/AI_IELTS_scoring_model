[SAMPLE_ANSWER]
Band 6
The process starts with clay being dug up using a metal digger. The clay is placed on a metal grid and crushed into smaller pieces. Water and sand are added, and the mixture is formed into bricks either by moulding or through a wire cutter. The bricks are dried and then heated in two kilns at different temperatures. Finally, they are cooled and packaged.

[OVERVIEW]
Clay is shaped, dried, burned and prepared for construction.
