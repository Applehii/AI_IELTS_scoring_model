The diagram shows the process of recycling glass bottles.

[SAMPLE_ANSWER]

Used glass bottles are first collected and separated by color. They are washed in a cleaning machine and then crushed into small fragments. These fragments are melted in a furnace and the liquid glass is poured into moulds to create new bottles. The finished bottles are inspected, packed and delivered to shops.

[OVERVIEW]
Old bottles are cleaned, melted and reshaped into new ones.
