The diagram illustrates the main stages in producing coffee. First, ripe coffee beans are harvested from the plants. They are then dried under the sun until they lose most moisture. After drying, the beans are roasted in a special machine and then ground into fine powder. Finally, the coffee powder is packed into containers and transported to shops.

[OVERVIEW]
Overall, the process involves harvesting, drying, roasting, grinding and packaging.
