Wood is cut into logs first. Then the logs are crushed and mixed with water to become pulp. This mixture is pressed and dried. After drying, it becomes sheets of paper. The paper is then rolled and packed.

[OVERVIEW]
Wood is turned into paper through pulping and drying.
