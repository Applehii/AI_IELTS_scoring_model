The pronounced compensation gaps carry significant implications for economic inequality, workforce distribution, and sectoral labor supply sustainability. Technology's premium attracts talent disproportionately, potentially creating skill shortages in essential services including education and healthcare. The retail sector's depressed compensation creates workforce retention challenges and service quality concerns.
