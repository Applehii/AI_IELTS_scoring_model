[SAMPLE_ANSWER]

The seaside village experienced noticeable development from 1990 to 2020. In 1990, the area mainly consisted of small houses near the beach and a single main road. By 2020, several new residential buildings had been constructed, and a hotel appeared along the coast. The beach was also upgraded with designated walking areas. Although the main road stayed the same, a new side road was introduced, providing better access to the eastern part of the village.

[OVERVIEW]
The village expanded with more housing and tourism facilities.
