LEXICAL RESOURCE 


Band 9:
- Uses a wide range of vocabulary with very natural and precise word choice.
- Skillful use of collocations and less common lexical items.
- Very rare inaccuracies.

Band 8:
- Uses a wide range of vocabulary with good flexibility.
- Occasional inaccuracies but meaning is never impeded.
- Good use of collocations.

Band 7:
- Sufficient range of vocabulary for the task.
- Some repetition or less precise word choice.
- Occasional errors but meaning is clear.

Band 6:
- Limited lexical range.
- Noticeable repetition.
- Errors in word choice may cause some strain for the reader.

Band 5 or below:
- Very limited vocabulary.
- Frequent repetition and inappropriate word choice.
- Errors often obscure meaning.
