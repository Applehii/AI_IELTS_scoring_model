COHERENCE & COHESION 

Band 9:
- Paragraphing is fully logical and effective.
- Ideas progress naturally and smoothly.
- Cohesive devices are used accurately and flexibly.

Band 8:
- Clear paragraphing with strong logical progression.
- Cohesive devices are well used but may be slightly repetitive.

Band 7:
- Logical paragraphing overall.
- Clear progression with some minor lapses.
- Adequate use of cohesive devices.

Band 6:
- Paragraphing is present but may be mechanical.
- Progression is uneven.
- Limited or repetitive use of cohesive devices.

Band 5 or below:
- Weak or unclear paragraphing.
- Poor logical flow.
- Very limited or incorrect cohesive devices.
