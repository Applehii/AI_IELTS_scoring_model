====================
TASK RESPONSE BAND RUBRIC (TR) – IELTS TASK 2
(TIER 1 – TASK-AGNOSTIC CORE CONDITIONS)
====================

Band 9:
- Fully addresses ALL REQUIRED elements of the task (as defined by the task type).
- A clear and consistent position is presented throughout, IF the task type requires a position.
- All main ideas are fully developed with clear explanation and relevant support.
- No irrelevant or off-task content is present.

Band 8:
- Addresses ALL REQUIRED elements of the task.
- A clear position is presented, IF the task type requires a position.
- Main ideas are well developed, though support may be less extended than Band 9.
- Minor lapses in focus may occur, but no clearly irrelevant content.

Band 7:
- Addresses ALL REQUIRED elements of the task, BUT:
  - A position may be unclear or inconsistently stated, IF required by the task type, OR
  - Some required ideas are developed only at a basic level.
- No required element is completely missing.
- Minor irrelevant content may appear but does not dominate.

Band 6:
- Partially addresses the task:
  - At least ONE required element is insufficiently addressed or only mentioned.
- A position may be unclear, inconsistent, or weak, IF required.
- Ideas are basic or repetitive, with limited explanation or support.
- Some irrelevant content may be present.

Band 5 or below:
- The task is NOT adequately addressed:
  - One or more REQUIRED elements are completely missing.
- A required position is missing or contradictory.
- Ideas are largely undeveloped or irrelevant.
- Off-task content may dominate the response.
