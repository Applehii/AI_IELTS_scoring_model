TASK ACHIVEMENT

Band 9:
- Overview highlights the most significant trends only.
- Data is grouped logically (not listed chronologically).
- Key comparisons are clearly made and fully accurate.

Band 8:
- Clear and well-developed overview.
- Mostly logical grouping.
- Comparisons are clear but not fully extended.

Band 7:
- Clear overview but may miss subtle trends.
- Mostly logical grouping of data.
- Comparisons are present but not always fully developed.

Band 6:
- Overview is present but generic or unclear.
- Data is often listed sequentially rather than grouped.
- Limited or basic comparisons.

Band 5 or below:
- Missing or unclear overview.
- Poor or no grouping of data.
- Very limited or inaccurate comparisons.
