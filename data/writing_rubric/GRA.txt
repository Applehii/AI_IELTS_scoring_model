GRAMMARTICAL RANGE AND ACCURACY

Band 9:
- Uses a wide range of complex sentence structures with full flexibility.
- Errors are very rare and difficult to spot.
- Grammar is consistently accurate.

Band 8:
- Uses a wide range of sentence structures.
- Occasional minor errors, but these do not affect communication.
- Good control of complex sentences.

Band 7:
- Uses a mix of simple and complex sentences.
- Some errors in complex structures, but meaning is clear.
- Errors do not impede communication.

Band 6:
- Uses a limited range of structures.
- Frequent errors in complex sentences.
- Some errors may reduce clarity.

Band 5 or below:
- Very limited range of sentence structures.
- Frequent grammatical errors.
- Errors often obscure meaning.
